# Poliwar
### A game about populations (and no wars)
## Premise
You are a newly ellected president in a country in which 3 races live: green, blue and red. Greens are *quite nice*. Reds cause *chaos*. You have **20** days until your presidency ends.
## Main goal
The goal of Poliwar is to achieve a state which you like. **Theres no set goal.**
## Ways of getting to said goal
Every day, you can choose from 1 of 3 random policies. After choosing a policy, press a to advance to the next day. You can also skip picking a policy and just press a without selecting anything.
